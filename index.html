What RocketSim Models

📐

Flight Physics

Full thrust, drag, and gravitational simulation using real Newtonian mechanics and atmospheric density calculations.

🌤️

Live Weather

Auto-fetches real-time temperature, humidity, pressure, and wind at your location to refine simulation accuracy.

⚖️

Stability Analysis

Estimates centre of pressure and centre of gravity from your fin and nose cone geometry to check flight stability.

🤖

AI Flight Analyst

After simulation, an AI assistant analyses your data and gives specific suggestions to improve altitude, stability, or motor choice.

✏️

Rocket Builder

Design your rocket visually with real-time SVG preview and send dimensions directly to the simulator.

🎬

Launch Visualiser

Watch a full animated simulation of your rocket's flight — side view and trajectory arc, synced to your exact data.

About the Creator

Built by Aarush

RocketSim was designed and built by Aarush, a Year 11 student at Doha College, Qatar, with a passion for aerospace engineering. This project combines applied physics, computational simulation, and software development — exploring the same fundamental principles that underpin real rocket engineering.

The simulator draws on Newtonian mechanics, atmospheric science, and aerodynamic theory to produce accurate flight predictions for model rockets ranging from small Estes motors to high-power H-class motors.
